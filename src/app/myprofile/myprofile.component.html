Tell us about yourself.

When I was 19 years old, I was taking a public speaking class where the Professor talked about the many faces we wear and it got me to thinking about how we pretend in the faces of others. Well, each day I aim to throw away more and more fake faces so that one day, the only face left is the authentic, original, unique, wild and free soul that has a face, known as Me.
